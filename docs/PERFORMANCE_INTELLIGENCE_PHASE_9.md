# Performance Intelligence Phase 9

Adds automatic insight generation for Head Office and franchise users.

## Included

- New route: `/performance/insights`
- Group-level insights for Head Office
- Franchise-specific insights
- Target risk detection
- Previous month decline detection
- Same month last year decline detection
- Consecutive growth/decline streaks
- Record-month detection
- Suggested decision actions

No database migration is required. Insights are calculated live from imported monthly figures, targets and leaderboard movement.
