# Performance Intelligence Phase 8

## Executive Dashboard

Phase 8 adds a Head Office decision centre at:

`/performance/executive`

It is cumulative on top of Phases 1-7.

## Included

- Executive KPI cards for Cash, Sales, Insurance Premiums, Joinings and Funerals.
- Total franchises measured for the selected month/year.
- Top performers list.
- Needs-attention list based on business health and KPI risk.
- Biggest climbers.
- Biggest droppers.
- Forecast / target risk table for KPIs below 90%.
- KPI decision summary table.
- Quick links to franchise detail pages, KPI pages and leaderboards.

## Target logic

The dashboard uses the shared Performance Intelligence engine and therefore respects:

- PDF/monthly import data.
- Manual targets.
- Fair growth bracket targets.
- Annual budget targets.
- Previous-month movement.
- Same-month-last-year comparison.

## Deployment

No new migration is required for Phase 8.

Push the files to the development branch and redeploy Render.
